About Pretzel.js

The first and foremost goal of pretzel is to create a portable, minimalistic webGL visualizing framework for 3D linear structures (i.e. biological chromosome). The library also provide a wide variety of utility to interact and examine the structures in high details.

Features

High level abstraction

Simple and straightforward APIs

Simulation and data generator
